Please add the following sound files to this directory:

1. hover.mp3 - A short, subtle hover sound for when users hover over the logo
   Recommended: A soft "whoosh" or "ping" sound (0.2-0.5 seconds)

2. ambient.mp3 - A low, continuous ambient background sound
   Recommended: A soft tech/digital ambiance (looping, 30-60 seconds)

3. click.mp3 - A quick click/interaction sound
   Recommended: A soft "click" or "tap" sound (0.1-0.3 seconds)

Sound files should be in MP3 format and kept small in size (under 100KB each) for fast loading.
Volume levels have been preset in the JavaScript:
- Ambient sound: 10% volume
- Hover sound: 20% volume
- Click sound: 30% volume
